Electric Picnic Tickets

All tickets are officially sold out. Please keep your eyes open for 2020 tickets!

You must be aged 18 years or over to attend this event, with the exception of children aged 12 and under. Children under 12 years of age do not need a ticket. All under 12's must be accompanied by a parent or guardian who is over 18 years old and this adult must remain on site throughout the weekend. Those wishing to stay in the 'Family Campsite' must have children 12 years or younger in the group. As in previous years 13-17 year olds are not permitted to attend even when accompanied by an adult. It is also not acceptable to turn up to the festival with children aged between 13-17 years old. Children in this age range will not gain free admission to the festival or otherwise.
